# [CHECK IT OUT ON MODRINTH](https://modrinth.com/modpack/silly-mc)

SillyMC is a modpack focused on expanding and overhauling vanilla content with newer, fresher mods, while also adding mods that add completely new features. Also it tries to put together some mods to try and make a cool modded experience on modern Minecraft, where there aren't as many content mods.

## Features

<details>
<summary>Adventure Mods</summary>

- [Wilder Wilds](https://modrinth.com/mod/wilder-wild) upgrades world generation.
- [Trailier Tales](https://modrinth.com/mod/trailier-tales) overhauls some archaeological structures and adds a new **Catacombs** dungeon.
- [Enderscapes](https://modrinth.com/mod/enderscape) overhauls the End dimension with new biomes, mobs, and additional features.
- [Mariposa](https://modrinth.com/mod/mariposa) adds a new, beautiful giant sequoia forest biome.
- [Friend and Foes](https://modrinth.com/mod/friends-and-foes) adds outvoted or forgotten mobs to Minecraft that serve as new challenges.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Building Mods</summary>

- [Accurate Block Placement](https://modrinth.com/mod/accurate-block-placement-reborn) makes block placement smarter by timing it for you.
- [Connectible Chains](https://modrinth.com/mod/connectiblechains) lets you attach chains between fences and walls.
- [Jake's Build Tools](https://modrinth.com/mod/jakes-build-tools) offers tools that make building easier.
- [Inferno](https://modrinth.com/mod/inferno) rekindles fire-related features while also adding new building blocks.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Combat/Equipment Mods</summary>

- [Combat Amenities](https://modrinth.com/mod/combat-amenities) tweaks small combat-related features and adds a back slot.
- [Nycto](https://modrinth.com/mod/nycto) allows you to turn into a **Vampire** or hunt them.
- [Farmer's Delight](https://modrinth.com/mod/farmers-delight-refabricated) adds knives and a wide variety of foods, increasing diversity in combat and gameplay.
- [Enchancement](https://modrinth.com/mod/enchancement) removes boring stat-increasing enchantments from vanilla and adds new ones that transform your weapons.
- [Enderscapes](https://modrinth.com/mod/enderscape) besides overhauling the End dimension, it also expands the arsenal of the Player.
- [Spears](https://modrinth.com/mod/accurate-spears) backports spears into the game.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Tech Mods</summary>

- [Create Fly](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-fly) is a higher-version port of Create.
- [Copper Hoppers](https://modrinth.com/mod/copper-hopper) and [Copper Pipes](https://modrinth.com/mod/simple-copper-pipes) add new sorting and item-transport blocks.
- [Wayfinder](https://modrinth.com/mod/wayfinder_rbn) adds simple but diegetic improvements to navigation.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Social Mods</summary>

- [Essential Mod](https://modrinth.com/mod/essential) is a mod that offers hosting worlds for free, chatting with friends, and so much more!
- [Signed Paintings](https://modrinth.com/mod/signed-paintings) lets you upload images using paintings.
- [Areas](https://modrinth.com/mod/areas) allows the creation of **custom regions, zones, and towns** with join and leave messages via a GUI.
- [Yet Another World Protector](https://modrinth.com/mod/yawp) is an admin tool that lets admins set up zones with specific rules.

</details>

<details>
<summary>QOL Mods</summary>

- [Comforts](https://modrinth.com/mod/comforts) adds sleeping bags and hammocks for portability and for turning night to day, respectively, without setting new spawn points.
- [InvMove](https://modrinth.com/mod/invmove) allows you to walk around while in inventories.
- [Respawnable Pets](https://modrinth.com/mod/respawnable-pets) allows pets to respawn after death when you next sleep, using a new item called the Etheric Gem.
- [RightClickHarvest](https://modrinth.com/mod/rightclickharvest) allows you to harvest crops with right-click.
- [Superb Steeds](https://modrinth.com/mod/superb-steeds) overhauls horses with various adjustments and training mechanics.
- [Stendhal](https://modrinth.com/mod/stendhal) offers new and enhanced visual utilities for books, signs, anvils, and in-game chat.
- [Trade Cycling](https://modrinth.com/mod/trade-cycling) lets you refresh villager trades without needing to break workstations.
- [Graves](https://modrinth.com/mod/universal-graves) protects your items in a grave after dying and prevents other players from accessing them for 10 minutes.
- Xaero's Map Mods adds a minimap and a world map. The modpack allows admins to disable cheaty features such as the entity radar or cave mode.

</details>

<details>
<summary>For Fun Mods</summary>

- [Controlify](https://modrinth.com/mod/controlify) adds controller support to the modpack.
- [Vivecraft](https://modrinth.com/mod/vivecraft) adds VR support to the modpack.
- Several Ambience mods such as [Particular](https://modrinth.com/mod/particular-reforged), [Wakes](https://modrinth.com/mod/wakes), [Explosive Enhancement](https://modrinth.com/mod/explosive-enhancement), and more.

</details>
